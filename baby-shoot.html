Faq

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best age for a baby photoshoot?
Newborn sessions are ideal within the first 15 days, while milestone shoots are popular at 3, 6, and 12 months.
How long does a baby photoshoot take?
Most baby sessions take between 1-3 hours, allowing plenty of time for feeding, cuddling, and comfort breaks.
Can parents and siblings join the session?
Absolutely! Family portraits with parents and siblings create beautiful memories and are included in many sessions.
Do you provide props and accessories?
Yes, we offer a selection of baby-friendly props, baskets, wraps, headbands, and themed accessories.
Where will the photoshoot take place?
Sessions can be conducted at our studio, your home, or an outdoor location based on your preference.
When will we receive our photos?
Professionally edited photographs are typically delivered within 1–2 weeks after the session.
