Frequently Asked Questions
What is the best age for a baby photoshoot?

For newborn photography, the best window is between 5 to 14 days. For toddler/milestone shoots, 6 to 9 months (when the baby sits independently) or 12 months (first birthday cake smash) are highly recommended stages!

Are baby costumes and props provided by you?

Yes! We maintain an extensive range of sanitized theme props, setups, and baby costumes. You can choose props and outfits during booking.

What steps are taken to ensure the baby's safety?

Your child's safety is our primary focus. All props are thoroughly sanitized before every shoot. The studio temperature is kept warm and comfortable, and our photographers are experienced in pacing the session patiently based on the baby's needs for feeding, rests, or sleep.
