Faq

Frequently Asked Questions

What types of events do you photograph?
We cover a wide range of social and corporate events, including birthday celebrations, housewarmings, puberty functions, ear piercings, baby showers, family reunions, corporate conferences, and product launches.
Do you offer customized packages for events?
Yes. Since every event differs in size, hours, and scope, we offer fully customized packages based on your requirements.
How many photographers will cover our event?
The number of crew members depends on the scale of the event. Small events can be handled by a single photographer, while large functions may require multiple photographers and videographers.
Do you cover multi-day events?
Yes. We cover multi-day events, such as family festivals, business workshops, and long traditional ceremonies, providing unified coverage throughout.
What is the turnaround time for receiving event photos?
Edited, high-resolution event photographs are delivered through our digital gallery within 1 to 2 weeks post-event. Express delivery options can be arranged if needed.
Do you travel outside Madurai for events?
Yes. While we are based in Madurai, we regularly travel across Tamil Nadu and neighboring regions to provide high-quality event photography services.
