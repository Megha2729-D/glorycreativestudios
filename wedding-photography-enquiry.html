Cinematic Wedding Storytellers

Glory Photography is a premier wedding photography team based in Madurai, Tamil Nadu. With over 5+ years of experience and 1000+ weddings covered, we are dedicated to capturing the genuine emotions, candid moments, and rich cultural traditions of your special day.

From candid portrait sessions to grand traditional ceremony coverages, drone angles, and elegant cinematic wedding films, we bring state-of-the-art visual style to your celebrations.

Frequently Asked Questions
When should we book our wedding photographer?

Wedding dates during peak seasons book out quickly. We recommend booking 4 to 6 months in advance to ensure our team is available for your dates.

Do you travel outside Madurai for weddings?

Yes! We regularly travel all over Tamil Nadu and neighboring states. Travel and basic accommodation charges are added for shoots outside Madurai.

How long does it take to deliver the photos and albums?

We deliver a teaser set of 20-30 edited photos within 1 week. The complete edited digital gallery is delivered within 4-5 weeks, and printed albums are shipped within 6-8 weeks after you select your photos.
