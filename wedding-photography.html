Faq

Frequently Asked Questions

How early should we book?
We recommend booking 3–6 months before your wedding date.
Do you travel for destination weddings?
Yes, we provide photography services across Tamil Nadu and destination locations.
Do you provide albums?
Yes, premium wedding albums are available with selected packages.
How many photos will we receive?
The number of photographs depends on the package and event duration. On average, couples receive 300–800 professionally edited images.
How long does it take to deliver the photos?
Wedding galleries are typically delivered within 2–4 weeks, depending on the scope of the event and editing requirements.
Do you offer candid photography?
Yes, candid photography is one of our specialties. We focus on capturing genuine emotions, natural interactions, and memorable moments throughout your wedding day.
Can we request specific shots or poses?
Absolutely. We encourage couples to share inspiration, special moments, family groupings, and must-have shots before the wedding day.
Do you provide cinematic wedding films?
Yes, we offer cinematic wedding videography, teaser reels, highlight films, and full wedding films as part of our wedding packages.
Do you cover multi-day wedding events?
Yes, we provide coverage for engagement ceremonies, mehendi, sangeet, receptions, and other pre- and post-wedding celebrations.
Do you provide drone photography and videography?
Yes, drone coverage is available for selected packages and venues, subject to local regulations and weather conditions.
How do we reserve our wedding date?
Your wedding date is secured once the booking agreement is completed and the advance payment is received.
