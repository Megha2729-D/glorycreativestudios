Faq

Frequently Asked Questions

How much does wedding photography cost in Madurai?
Pricing depends on the number of events, coverage duration, photography style, and deliverables. We offer fully customised packages — contact us for a quote tailored to your wedding.
Do you provide both photography and videography?
Yes. We offer complete wedding coverage including candid photography, traditional photography, cinematic films, and drone footage — all handled by one professional team.
How early should we book a wedding photographer?
We recommend booking 3 to 6 months before your wedding date, especially during peak season (November to February), to secure your preferred date.
Will you guide us during the shoot?
Yes. We provide gentle, natural direction when needed — ensuring you feel at ease in front of the camera without your moments feeling staged.
Do you travel outside Madurai?
Yes. We cover weddings throughout Tamil Nadu and are available for destination weddings across India.
When will we receive our wedding photographs?
Delivery timelines are confirmed at the time of booking. All edited photographs and films are delivered within the agreed schedule — no delays.
