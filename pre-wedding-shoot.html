Faq

Frequently Asked Questions

What is a pre-wedding photoshoot?
A pre-wedding photoshoot is a couple photography session taken a few months before the wedding day, focusing on capturing the chemistry and romance of the couple in casual or styled outfits.
When is the best time to schedule our pre-wedding shoot?
We recommend scheduling the shoot 2 to 3 months before your wedding. This allows ample time for editing, album design, or creating invitations and slideshows.
Do you recommend locations for the shoot?
Yes. We guide couples on selecting locations based on their vision, whether it's heritage temples in Madurai, a serene beach, scenic hills, or custom set studios.
What should we wear for the photoshoot?
We suggest coordinated outfits (either complementary colors or matching themes) such as smart-casuals, elegant gowns, or traditional sarees/sherwanis. Avoiding busy patterns helps focus on your faces.
How long does a pre-wedding shoot take?
Typically, a pre-wedding shoot takes 3 to 6 hours, depending on the number of locations, outfit changes, and package options selected.
Can we use the photos for our wedding save-the-date invitations?
Yes, absolutely! Many couples use these photos and videos for digital save-the-dates, invitation templates, social media posts, and prints displayed at their reception.
